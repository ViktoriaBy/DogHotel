About Us

Your pets are more than just companion animals, they're full-fledged members of your family. You'd take them everywhere with you if you could, but that's not possible or practical. That's where we come in. We're Susis Dog Hotel, and for years, we've provided top-notch pet boarding you won't find anywhere else in Rockwall County.

Bring your dog to us for short or long-term boarding. We're confident you and your pets will agree that Susis Dog Hotel is the place to be. We can even pick your pet up and bring them home. Every breed an every mutt is welcome!
